Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Vocal Bazar ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile applications and services, including:

  • Vocal Bazar – Customer food delivery and local services app
  • VB Rider – Delivery agent app
  • VB Vendor – Vendor / merchant management app

All three applications operate under the same platform and backend services, and this Privacy Policy applies to users of all these apps.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app and user role, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number
  • Location Information: Delivery address, pickup location, or service area
  • Order & Task Information: Orders, delivery tasks, order status, and history
  • Vendor Information: Shop name, menu items, pricing, and availability

How We Use Your Information

  • To process food orders and local service requests
  • To assign and manage deliveries through VB Rider
  • To enable vendors to manage orders using VB Vendor
  • To verify users via phone number and OTP authentication
  • To communicate order, delivery, and service updates
  • To provide customer and partner support

Payments

Payments are handled using Cash on Delivery or UPI on Delivery. We do not collect or store sensitive payment information such as debit/credit card details.

Checkout Process

When placing an order through the Vocal Bazar platform, users can review their selected items, delivery address, and total order amount before confirming the order.

  • Users must verify the order details before confirming checkout.
  • Once an order is placed, it is forwarded to the respective vendor for preparation.
  • The platform may assign a delivery agent through the VB Rider app to complete the delivery.
  • Payment is currently collected at the time of delivery via Cash or UPI.

Checkout & Payment Flow

During checkout from the cart page, users are presented with available payment options before confirming their order.

  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Users can choose to pay the order amount directly to the delivery agent at the time of delivery.
  • Online Payment: Users can choose to pay online using supported payment methods.

If the user selects Online Payment, they will be securely redirected to a third-party Payment Gateway page to complete the transaction.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Orders placed through the Vocal Bazar platform may be cancelled under certain circumstances depending on the order status.

  • Orders may be cancelled before the vendor begins preparation.
  • Once an order has been prepared or dispatched for delivery, cancellation may not be possible.
  • If an order cannot be fulfilled due to vendor issues, delivery problems, or service unavailability, users may be eligible for a refund.

Currently, refunds are processed manually by our support team within 3–7 working days.
