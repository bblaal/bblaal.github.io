Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Vocal Bazar ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects user information when you use our mobile applications and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Phone number, name (provided during ordering)
  • Location Information: Delivery address and location selected by the user
  • Order Information: Items ordered, order history, and delivery details

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver food orders
  • To verify users using phone number and OTP authentication
  • To communicate order-related updates
  • To provide customer support

Payments

Payments are handled using Cash on Delivery or UPI on Delivery. We do not collect or store sensitive payment information such as card details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers. User information is shared only as required to fulfill orders (for example, with delivery partners or restaurants).

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using secure communication protocols (HTTPS).

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and to comply with legal or accounting requirements.

Account and Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data by contacting us at:

vocal.bazaar@gmail.com

Please include the registered phone number used in the app. Requests are processed within 7–14 working days.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not designed specifically for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us
