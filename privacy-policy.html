Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Vocal Bazar ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile applications and services, including:

  • Vocal Bazar – Customer food delivery and local services app
  • VB Rider – Delivery agent app
  • VB Vendor – Vendor / merchant management app

All three applications operate under the same platform and backend services, and this Privacy Policy applies to users of all these apps.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app and user role, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number
  • Location Information: Delivery address, pickup location, or service area
  • Order & Task Information: Orders, delivery tasks, order status, and history
  • Vendor Information: Shop name, menu items, pricing, and availability

How We Use Your Information

  • To process food orders and local service requests
  • To assign and manage deliveries through VB Rider
  • To enable vendors to manage orders using VB Vendor
  • To verify users via phone number and OTP authentication
  • To communicate order, delivery, and service updates
  • To provide customer and partner support

Payments

Payments are handled using Cash on Delivery or UPI on Delivery. We do not collect or store sensitive payment information such as debit/credit card details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third-party advertisers. Data is shared only between customers, delivery agents, and vendors as required to fulfill orders and services.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. All data transmitted between our apps and servers is encrypted using HTTPS.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or to comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Account and Data Deletion

Users of Vocal Bazar, VB Rider, or VB Vendor can request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting:

vocal.bazaar@gmail.com

Please include the registered phone number and app name. Requests are processed within 7–14 working days.

Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
