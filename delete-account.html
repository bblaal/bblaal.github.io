Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data for apps published by Vocal Bazar.

How to Request Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and personal data by sending an email to:

vocal.bazaar@gmail

Please include the registered phone number used in the app so we can verify your request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information (phone number)
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Order history associated with the account

Data That May Be Retained

  • Transaction records required for legal, accounting, or regulatory purposes

Account and data deletion requests are processed within 7–14 working days.

If you have questions regarding data deletion, please contact us at vocal.bazaar@gmail.
