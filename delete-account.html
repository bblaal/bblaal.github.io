Account and Data Deletion

This page applies to apps published by Vocal Bazar.

How to Request Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data by sending an email to:

Email: vocal.bazaar@gmail.com

Please include the registered phone number used in the app so we can verify your request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information (phone number)
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Order history associated with the account

Data That May Be Retained

  • Transaction records required for legal or accounting purposes

Data deletion requests will be processed within 7–14 working days.
